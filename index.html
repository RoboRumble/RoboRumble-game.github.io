Battlegrounds

The Five Arenas

Each arena isolates a core electrical engineering constraint. Master one, master them all.

🌏

Regolith Flats

Power Management

Open lunar terrain. No tricks, no shortcuts — just you and your power budget. The proving ground where every milliamp matters.

🔧

The Conduit

Wiring Reliability

Narrow sub-surface corridors filled with vibration. Loose connections fail here. Only the cleanest wiring survives.

☀

Helios Shelf

Sensor Shielding

Peak solar exposure on the crater rim. Radiation floods your sensors — shield them or fight blind.

🌑

Shadowside

Battery Efficiency

Permanent shadow. Zero solar. Your battery is all you have — manage draw or shut down before the final round.

🔥

The Forge

Thermal Load

Volcanic vent proximity. Extreme heat degrades components in real time. Cooling strategy is your weapon here.

Core Philosophy

Learning Through Failure

Invalid builds aren't rejected — they enter combat and visibly malfunction. A reversed motor spins the wrong way. An overloaded circuit pops a fuse mid-fight. You don't read an error message — you watch your robot fail, understand why, and fix it. Failure is the best teacher on the moon.

🛠

Programming

Dual-Layer Programming

Start with Scratch-style blocks, peek at the real code underneath. A two-way bridge so the training wheels come off naturally.

🤖
